ABOUT US

Welcome to the Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University Hiking Club! Established with a passion for exploration and a love for nature, our club is a vibrant community of outdoor enthusiasts. Our mission is to promote the joy of hiking while fostering a sense of camaraderie among members. Whether you're a seasoned trekker or just starting out on your hiking journey, we welcome individuals from all backgrounds and skill levels to join us in discovering the breathtaking landscapes surrounding Ankara.

At the heart of our club is a commitment to adventure and discovery. Each month, we organize a variety of hikes ranging from leisurely nature walks to challenging mountain ascents. Led by experienced guides, our excursions take us through picturesque trails, dense forests, and majestic peaks, offering unforgettable experiences and opportunities to connect with nature. Beyond hiking, we also host workshops, social events, and volunteer initiatives to further enrich our members' outdoor experiences.

Joining the Ankara Yıldırım Beyazıt University Hiking Club means more than just exploring the great outdoors—it's about forging lasting friendships and creating memories that will last a lifetime. Whether it's the thrill of conquering a new summit or the tranquility of a peaceful forest stroll, our club provides a supportive and inclusive environment for all to enjoy. Come hike with us and embark on a journey of adventure, discovery, and community!
