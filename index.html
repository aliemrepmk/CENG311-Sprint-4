EXPLORE    WITH AYBU


Welcome to the largest and most active hiking club at the university! We are passionate about exploring the great outdoors and delight in introducing others to the breathtaking natural beauty of our area. Our meticulously planned hikes cater to a variety of skill levels, ensuring that everyone can find a suitable adventure. We are always excited to welcome new members, so whether you're lacing up your first pair of hiking boots or have a collection of well-worn pairs, we invite you to join us on the trail and share in the joy of outdoor exploration!
